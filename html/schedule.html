schedule of breastfeeding classes

NMC speakers teach free breastfeeding classes at various Bay Area locations. Classes, which are about two hours long, go over the benefits and how-to’s of breastfeeding as well as covering related issues commonly faced by new mothers. Class content repeats, so choose only one of the following dates.

Please sign up for Bay Area classes using our online form. If you are unable to sign up online, you may call 650 327-MILK (6455), or email speakers@nursingmothers.org to RSVP.
