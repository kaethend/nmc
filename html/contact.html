contact nmc

For breastfeeding information and advice in the San Francisco Bay Area, please call 650 327-MILK (6455). Callers from Santa Cruz County should call 831 688-3954.

email

Unfortunately, we are not able to offer breastfeeding counseling via email at this time. For other business, you may use one of the email addresses below.

  • For general questions or organizational information, contact info@nursingmothers.org
  • To inquire about becoming a member or training to become a counselor, contact membership@nursingmothers.org
  • For pump rentals, or pump and product sales, contact pumpsales@nursingmothers.org
  • To RSVP for a breastfeeding class or to schedule a breastfeeding class or speaker for your organization, contact speakers@nursingmothers.org (Bay Area) or scspeakers@nursingmothers.org (Santa Cruz County).
  • To request brochures, contact communityrelations@nursingmothers.org

mail

Written correspondence for theSan Francisco Bay Area chapter may be sent to po box 5024, San Mateo, CA 94402-0024.

Mail for the Santa Cruz County chapter should be addressed to po box 66634, Scotts Valley, CA 95067.
